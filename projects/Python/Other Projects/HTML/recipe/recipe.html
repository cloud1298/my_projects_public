Recipe

Chocolate cake

‹‹ Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe. Enjoy! ››

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).

2

In a large bowl, whisk together the sugar, black chocolate, and butter until smooth.

3

In a separate bowl, cream the eggs until frothy.

4

Add the flour and cooled eggs mixture to the bowl with the sugar mixture, stirring well to combine.

5

Gently fold in the dry ingredients until just combined.

6

Pour the batter into a greased 9x13 inch (23.3 cm x 30.4 cm) cake pan.

7

Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted.
